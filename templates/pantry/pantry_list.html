About Your Pantry

What is the Pantry?

Your pantry helps you track the ingredients you have available. This allows us to show you recipes that you can make with what you have on hand.

Related Ingredients

Adding related ingredients helps you discover more recipes. For example, if you have "olive oil", you might want to add "extra virgin olive oil" as a related ingredient. This way, you'll see recipes that use either type of olive oil.

Tips
  • Add ingredients you commonly use
  • Include related ingredients to see more recipe options
  • Keep your pantry updated to get the most relevant recipe suggestions
